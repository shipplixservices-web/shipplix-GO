HOW TO UPLOAD YOUR REAL PDF:
==========================

1. Rename your PDF file to:
   african-export-blueprint.pdf

2. Upload it to this "public" folder. In the AI Studio file explorer on the left:
   - Hover over this "public" folder.
   - Click the options (three dots) or right-click.
   - Choose "Upload File..." and select your PDF file.

3. Once uploaded, the file path must be:
   /public/african-export-blueprint.pdf

4. That's it! The "Download Blueprint" button on the website will automatically detect your uploaded PDF and download it directly instead of the text fallback.
